Getting There

Milford Sound can be accessed by road, air, or sea. Most visitors take a scenic drive from Queenstown or Te Anau, or book a scenic flight offering amazing aerial views.

Boat Tours

Daily cruises run throughout the year. Choose between nature cruises, overnight tours, and kayaking adventures. It is advised to book in advance during peak seasons.

Booking Information

  • Tour Operators: RealNZ, Southern Discoveries
  • Prices: Vary by tour type, starting from NZD $60
  • Website: realnz.com

Best Time to Visit

Milford Sound is stunning year-round, but spring and summer (October to March) provide longer daylight and calmer waters. Rainfall is common but adds to the dramatic waterfalls.
