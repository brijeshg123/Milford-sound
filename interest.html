Mitre Peak

One of the most iconic mountains in New Zealand, Mitre Peak rises sharply from the waters of the fjord, offering breathtaking views and incredible photo opportunities.

Image source: Department of Conservation

Stirling Falls

This powerful waterfall plunges 151 meters and is one of only two permanent waterfalls in the fjord. Boat tours take visitors close enough to feel the mist.

Image source: newzealand.com
